Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions published by X-Business-Factory, including XVelocity, ThreadDigest, QuickVerify, PureSnap, ReplyShield, AuthTag, RealTag, CalmReplies, SeenMark, SilentPin, MarkVault, DupeGuard, FollowPrime, KeyFlow, VoiceSkim, DMVault, MicroMomentum, SelfSearch, ResetPulse, QuotePeek, ActionEcho, MediaBoost, OriginPure, PostMirror, SmoothForge, LegacyX, UILock, EmojiPure, SFWSafe, and AnchorFeed.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Our extensions do not transmit, store, or share any information about you or your browsing activity to any server, third party, or external service.

2. Local Storage

Some extensions save your preferences (such as on/off toggles and settings) locally in your browser using the browser's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Permissions

Each extension requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • storage — to save your local preferences.
  • declarativeNetRequest (XVelocity only) — to block ads and trackers at the network level.
  • activeTab — to read content on the current tab when you click the extension.
  • debugger (PureSnap only) — to capture screenshots of the current tab.

No extension reads, transmits, or logs the content of pages you visit.

4. Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The Pro upgrade is handled via Gumroad, a third-party payment processor. Please review Gumroad's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

5. License Keys

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is stored locally in your browser. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our extensions are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our extensions after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
